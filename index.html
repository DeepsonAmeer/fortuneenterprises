Our Business Model

What we do, exactly.

Fortunes Enterprises is a consumer goods wholesale distribution company. We do not manufacture products. We purchase finished consumer goods in large quantities from FMCG manufacturers and authorised suppliers, store them in our warehouses, and resell them at wholesale prices to businesses that need them.

01

We Buy in Bulk from Manufacturers

Fortunes Enterprises purchases consumer goods — including dairy products, beverages, food items, and household products — directly from manufacturers and authorised distributors in large quantities.

02

We Store in Our Warehouses

All purchased goods are received, inspected, and stored in our organised warehouses. We maintain stock across multiple branches to ensure consistent availability for our customers.

03

We Sell Wholesale to Retailers & Traders

We sell these goods at wholesale prices to retailers, supermarkets, provision stores, pharmacies, kiosks, and open market traders who stock their shelves and sell directly to end consumers.

04

We Deliver to Our Customers

Orders placed by our retail customers are fulfilled from our warehouse stock and delivered to them via our own fleet or collected in-person from our depots.

Company Profile

Nigeria's consumer goods wholesale distributor.

Fortunes Enterprises is a registered consumer goods wholesale distribution company based in Nigeria. Our business activity is the bulk purchase of fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) — including dairy, beverages, food products, and household items — from manufacturers and authorised suppliers, and the subsequent wholesale resale of those goods to retailers, provision stores, supermarkets, and open market traders.

We do not sell directly to individual consumers. Our customers are businesses and traders who purchase from us in quantity to supply their own customers. We operate from organised warehouses, maintain multi-branch stock across depots, and run a tracked delivery fleet to fulfil customer orders.
